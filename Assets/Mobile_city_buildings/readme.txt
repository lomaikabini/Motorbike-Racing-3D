This package contains 15 different types of very low poly city-buildings
optimized for mobile-development or as background decoration.
Some of them come with different texture sets and object variations.

All objects come with predefined colliders.

Just drag the prefabs into your scene and arrange them as you like.

You may also want to have a look at the example scene that contains all the prefabs.

All buildings are optimized for mobile devices.
